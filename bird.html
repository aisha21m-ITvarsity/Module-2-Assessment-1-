THE BIRD HOUSE

Nestled amidst the lush foliage of the zoo's atmosphere, the bird house stands as a vibrant sanctuary, beckoning visitors into a realm of wonder. A symphony of chirps and melodies greets guests as they enter, surrounded by intricately crafted nests and perches, each tailored to accommodate the diverse inhabitants within. Come visit the bird house to see birds of every color and kind, as well as descriptions of them all so you can learn more about each of them!

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

The Bird House is opposite the monkey trail, in the center of the zoo.
